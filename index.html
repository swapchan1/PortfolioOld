ABOUT ME

Hi! I'm a UX Designer.

I am interested in sketching and designing anything – everything!

I am Swapnil Chandra and I am an HCI graduate student at IUPUI and I hold an undergraduate degree in Computer Science that enables me to think creatively but in the domains of what is possible.

I believe in pushing the boundaries of the box to find the best solution rather than only thinking outside the box. With this mindset, a strong logical and creative outlook I strive to design software in a way that will reach out and delight every person who interacts with it.

My first approach to any problem is: Pencil and Paper. I enjoy sketching and putting my thoughts on paper to get the required solution till I finalize it. With my logic, my strong knowledge and skillset I have built through my graduate and undergraduate courses, I wish to grow and gain as much exposure as possible to become a better designer. I aim to build a connection between technology and people.

If my skillset is what you are looking for, call me up (314-201-0701) or drop me an email (swapchan@iupui.edu).

WHAT CAN I DO?

User centered thinking

Throughout my childhood, I enjoyed teaching and helping my mother whenever she needed guidance while she used her mobile. While I helped, I studied her, how she thought, what she would have liked. This led me to think about how I could change the interface of the software easier to use without changing its features. This became my passion, thinking about creating easier ways to interact with technology for all users (not just computer engineers like me!)

Story-telling

As you will notice, just like my second-grade English teacher, I love narrating stories! This indirectly gives me a strong hold on personas, allowing me to easily imagine a “somebody” to represent a target audience!

Logical thinking

I loved doing Sudoku with my grandfather since my 3rd grade. This gradually built in me, a strong logical thinking approach!

Quick grasping ability

Trying out new things has always been my aim in life. I have tried and quickly learnt many skills ranging from guitar and sketching to karate and public speaking. I believe in being – Jack of all trades, master of ONE.

Sketching/Prototyping

The first thing I do if I have an idea is to sketch it out. Penning an idea and then building it from scratch on it provides me with a satisfaction that I have covered all areas of that idea and gives me room to think about more.

UX design + UX research

I truly believe in the UX design life cycle – Empathize to Test. I crave to contribute in each phase and dedicate myself to a product from the start to the finish. User-centered thinking gives me strong passion for UX research and a desire to sketch provides me with the passion for UX design. Therefore, I consider myself an all-rounder in this field.

Development

Development has logic, therefore I chose to pursue Computer Science as my undergraduate. 4 years of CS, taught me JAVA and C++ and many other languages and it gave me the foundation to learn new ones. This allows me to understand a developer and talk in their language and not design irrationally.

Critiquing

Critiquing is not only fault finding for me. For me it means that the product is already well built, it just needs the “X”- factor. Give me some time and I will find that X-Factor for the product.
